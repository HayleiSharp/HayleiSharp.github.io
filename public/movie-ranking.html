Haylei's Movie Rankings

Started April 24, 2022

1. Howl's Moving Castle

Of course, my first pick had to be the Studio Ghibli timeless classic "Howl's Moving Castle." With
beautiful, hand-drawn imagery, a chilling live orchestra, passionate voice actors, and compelling
characters, it's hard not to enjoy this masterpiece by Hayao Miyazaki.

2. Bullet Train

Taking a sharp shift in directions, my second favorite movie at the moment would go to the 2022 movie
"Bullet Train" directed by David Leitch. What starts as a simple dark-comedy spy movie takes a sudden
change and then becomes the funniest most unfortunate sequence of events. Throughout the movie,
various characters are introduced with their own motives all meeting together on a japanese bullet train.
Overall, the movie is hilariously dry-humored with an excellent casting.

3. Princess and the Frog

Continuing the world's most unorganized movie ranking, my next movie of all time is the beautiful
Disney movie "The Princess and the Frog." I've watched this movie about a trillion gazillion times, and
even then it never gets old. Anika Noni Rose's performance as Tiana is absolutely breathtaking and
genuinely gives me chills every time. It's hard not to fall in love with all the bizarre characters, from
a lovestruck firefly, to an extravagent crocodile who just wants to play jazz, to our hard-working and
ambitious heroine. Fair warning, though. These catchy, angelic tuned songs will 100% get stuck in your
head.
